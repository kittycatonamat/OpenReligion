Peoples environments were different, the tools we have now were not available. There were no smart drugs, ways of scientifically validating and verifying the advice, ways of collaborating on writing the texts which are as efficient as the ones we have now. The texts have been modified for political reasons and by human error when copying, translating and reciting to others orally.
